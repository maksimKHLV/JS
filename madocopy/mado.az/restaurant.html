MADO Restaurant Chain has been operating in Azerbaijan since 2010. MADO, which currently has 5 offices, opened its first restaurant in the heart of Baku, on the street of Mardanov brothers.

Three branches are located in the city center and two - in shopping centers (Ganjlik Mall and 28 Mall).
Our main goal is to open MADO Restaurants Chain in many regions of Azerbaijan and to discover the true taste of ice cream in all regions.
